WELCOME TO HIVEMIND

What is HiveMind?

The goal of HiveMind is to remove all the hard work from finding a group of like-minded nerds. Students can join an online “hive” of their course, where they advertise their note-taking skills to their classmates with the hopes of being recruited into a “hive”, or create their very own hive. A hive is a group of students who have forged an academic alliance—they can collaborate by uploading notes or discussing difficult topics in forums to become a super-powered study group.
